MY BEST CITIES

Venice

Venice,the capital of northern Italy's Veneto region,is built on more than 100 small islands in a lagoon in the Adriatic Sea.it has no roads,just canals including the Grand Canal thoroughfare-lined with renaissance and gothic palaces.venice also known as the "city of canls","The Floating City" and "Serenissima" is arguably one of Italys most pictureque cities.

Paris

Paris,France's capital,is a major European city and a global center for art,fashion,gastronomy and culture.its 19th-century cityscape is crissrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and 12th-century,gothic notre-dame cathedral,the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutigues along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honore.
